Design a system for FCA refugee support program.

The system has two components, landing page and beneficiary registration form.

The system must have two functional ends, Frontend and Backend. 
 - Frontend includes UI components, form validation and form submission.
 - Backend includes database, models, routes.

Validation Rules:
- Fields First name, Last name and  Place of birth are required and each must be at least 2 characters long
- Date of birth must be before date of registration.
- Gender is a radio buttons arranged horizontally whose value is female by default.
- Date of joining settlement camp must be after date of registration.
- Nationality, Marital status, and Settlement camp are selectable fields whose options are as below respectively.
  
  - Nationality:
    - Ugandan
    - Kenyan
    - Tanzanian
    - Burundian
    - Rwandese
    - Somali
    - South Sudanese

  - Marital status:
    - Single
    - Married
    - Divorced
    - Widowed
    - Separated

  - Settlement camp:
    - Gulu settlement camp
    - Arua settlement camp
    - Mbarara settlement camp
    - Kasese settlement camp
    - Busia settlement camp
    - Mbale settlement camp
    - Kigezi settlement camp

- A form should not be submitted if the fields are either empty or invalid
- On a successful submission of the form, fields and field boarders must be reset to default and a success alert must popup at the top of the form.
- On closing the success alert box, the form and all its fields resets to default.
