Welcome to FCA Refugee Support Program

We are committed to providing comprehensive support and assistance to refugees in their journey towards safety, dignity, and self-reliance. Our registration system helps us better understand and serve the needs of our beneficiaries across various settlement camps.

Our Support Services

Comprehensive assistance for refugees across multiple settlement camps

Emergency Relief

Immediate assistance with food, shelter, and basic necessities for newly arrived refugees.

Healthcare Services

Access to medical care, mental health support, and health education programs.

Education & Training

Educational opportunities and vocational training programs for skill development.

Legal Support

Legal assistance, documentation support, and advocacy for refugee rights.

Livelihood Programs

Economic empowerment through microfinance, business training, and employment opportunities.

Community Integration

Programs to facilitate integration with host communities and cultural orientation.
